BERNARD — Canonical ASCII/ANSI Art
====================================
HUMMBL Hummingbird Mascot | Terminal Core Aesthetic | Neo-ANSI Revival
Render with: cat bernard_canonical.txt

Palette: I = \033[38;5;78m (IRIDESCENT GREEN)  |  A = \033[38;5;208m (AMBER beak)
        W = \033[38;5;15m  (WHITE eyes)       |  M = \033[38;5;201m (MAGENTA throat)
        D = \033[38;5;245m (DIM wing blur)    |  C = \033[38;5;81m  (CYAN wingtip)
        0 = \033[0m  (RESET)

Bernard is the HUMMBL hummingbird. He hovers, pollinates, and departs.
He notices before he speaks. He never pushes.

================================================================================
ANATOMY GUIDE — Why This Reads as a Hummingbird
================================================================================

BEAK        >              ← Long, slender, forward-facing. The offer.

EYES      .-(o o)-.        ← Large for the head size. Watchful.

BODY      (   ===   )      ← Compact, iridescent throat (magenta).
              ~~             Wing blur lines show the hovering motion.

TAIL        \___/ \___/     ← Forked or rounded, stabilizing.

WING BLUR   ~ ~            ← Hummingbirds beat 50-80x/second. In ASCII,
                             ~ = blur, / \ = spread, . . = still.

================================================================================
COMPOSITION RULES
================================================================================
1. Beak always points right → forward momentum, offering
2. Eyes:  o → O → -        (normal → alert → closed/resting)
3. Wing blur intensity: ~~ → ~ ~ → . . → / \  (hover→slow→still→spread)
4. Body tilt: horizontal = neutral, \> = celebrating/looping
5. Color hierarchy: Beak (amber) → Eyes (white) → Body (green) → Wings (dim)
6. Bernard hovers alone in HUMMBL contexts; rides above CRAB in fleet contexts
7. Never larger than CRAB when both appear — pollinator, not predator

================================================================================
USAGE NOTES
================================================================================
- For 16-color fallback: 78→2 (green), 208→3 (yellow), 15→7 (white),
  201→5 (magenta), 245→7 (dim), 81→6 (cyan)
- All art designed for 80-column terminals minimum
- The tiny mark (3 lines) fits in a tmux status bar or sidebar indicator
- The medium mark (12 lines) requires 14 rows of vertical space
- Bernard replaces the plain ◉ circle in founder-mode sidebar chrome
- In bus messages, Bernard appears before CRAB (he notices first)
